Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information you voluntarily provide:

  • PDF files you upload for conversion

  • Basic profile information (name, profile picture)

Information we collect automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Usage data: number of conversions, file types, usage times
  • Approximate IP address (for general geographic analysis)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing Conversions: To convert your PDF files to Word format with high accuracy

You have the right to accept or reject personalized advertisements upon first launch of the app, and you can change this setting at any time from the app settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Files are automatically deleted after 24 hours

  • We conduct periodic security reviews to ensure your data safety

6. App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read PDF files and save converted Word files

You can manage these permissions at any time from your device settings.
